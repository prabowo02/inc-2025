\psection{Input}
Input begins with an integer $N$ ($1 \le N \le 100$). The next line contains $N$ integers: $A_i$ ($0 \le A_i \le 100$) representing the given array $A$.


\psection{Output}
Output in a line two integers (separated by a single space), the smallest and the largest integers in the given array, respectively.

\exmp{1}{
5\\
4 1 2 6 3
}{
1 6
}

\textit{Explanation of Sample 1:} The smallest integer in the given array is $1$, while the largest integer is $6$.

\exmp{2}{
2\\
9 9
}{
9 9
}
